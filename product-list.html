Serums are typically lightweight and have a thin, watery consistency that allows them to be easily absorbed into the skin.

Applying serum to your face can be a great way to deliver concentrated skincare ingredients that target specific concerns. Here are the general steps to apply serum to your face:

1. Cleanse your face: Start by washing your face with a gentle cleanser and patting it dry with a clean towel. This will ensure that your skin is clean and free of any dirt, oil, or makeup that can interfere with the absorption of the serum.

2. Apply toner (optional): If you use a toner as part of your skincare routine, apply it after cleansing your face and before applying serum. This will help to balance your skin's pH and prepare it for the serum.

3. Dispense the serum: Dispense a small amount of serum onto the palm of your hand. Usually, one or two pumps are enough to cover your entire face.

4. Apply the serum: Using your fingertips, gently pat the serum onto your face, starting from the center and moving outwards. Be sure to avoid the eye area.

5. Massage it in: Use a circular motion to massage the serum into your skin. This will help to improve blood circulation and maximize absorption.

6. Let it absorb: Allow the serum to absorb into your skin for a few minutes before applying any other skincare products.

7. Follow up with moisturizer: After the serum has absorbed into your skin, apply your regular moisturizer to lock in the hydration and nutrients from the serum.

Remember to read the instructions on the serum packaging as some serums may have specific application instructions. Additionally, if you have sensitive skin, do a patch test on a small area of your skin first to ensure that the serum does not cause any adverse reactions.
